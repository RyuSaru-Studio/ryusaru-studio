About RyuSaru Studio

RyuSaru Studio is a beginner-friendly indie game studio focused on learning through making. The goal is to build small, complete projects, try new ideas, and keep moving forward one game at a time.

Values

How the studio approaches development

Learn by building

Each project is a chance to practice real game development skills in a hands-on way.

Small scope

Clear boundaries help ideas stay manageable, focused, and easier to finish.

Finished prototypes

Even simple projects should feel complete, playable, and worth sharing.

Playful ideas

Creativity, curiosity, and charm are part of every experiment and release.
